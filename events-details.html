West Village | Brisbane Property Exhibition

DATE:

7th -9th September 2018 (Friday - Sunday)

Time:

11:00 – 19:00

Address:

Suite 1307-1309, 13/F, Leighton Centre, 77 Leighton Road, Causeway Bay, Hong Kong

West Village is a picturesque residential and lifestyle village set against the historic backdrop of the old Peters Ice Cream Factory. Idyllic laneways, state-of-the-art apartments, green spaces, and boutique cafes, bars and speciality shops set the standard for a lifestyle that's unique to Brisbane.

Live just one kilometre from the CBD in the heart of West End’s vibrant Boundary Street precinct and discover an urban village rich in culture and steeped in history, with countless personal touches. Experience a perfect balance between heritage buildings, lush parklands, original art, on-site childcare, car sharing, exclusive event spaces, a luxurious private pool, an outdoor cinema, and Brisbane’s best dining and entertainment on your doorstep.
